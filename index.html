What is Cyberpunk 2077

"Cyberpunk 2077" is a narrative-driven RPG game developed and published by CD Projekt Red. Set in the dystopian world, "Night City" is a place filled with disparity where the mega corporations holds immense power, and society is divided by the ultra-rich and the oppressed masses. Night City is a hub of cybernetics where individuals often "chrome" themselves. In other words, citizens often augment themselves to either upgrade for more power or to simply just change their appearance. However, this can come at a great cost as chroming yourself can lead to high levels of violence and cosmetic addictions. The city constantly suffers from gang violence and corporate warfare. "Cyberpunk 2077" vividly captures the harsh reality of these conflicts, immersing players in a world where danger lurks around every corner.

Cyberpunk Trivia

What is the Blackwall?
The blackwall is considered a band aid solution, due to the fact that the blackwall itself is actually an AI, masquerading as a firewall, that's designed to keep rogue AI from breaching the net and netrunners from trying to go far into the net. Netrunners and even NetWatch themselves have come to the conclusion that the blackwall could also one day go rogue.
Where did the term "Samurai" come from in Cyberpunk 2077?
The term Samurai was coined by fellow band member Nancy Hartley after she mocked Johnny Silverhand and Kerry Eurodyne. Samurai means that someone is willing to die for a cause that's morally wrong.
Why did Johnny Silverhand help create Samurai?
Serving in the US Army exposed him to the corruptions of the megacorporations as well as losing his arm and losing a comrade.
